Amazing Facts

Habitat

Hyacinth Macaws live in South America, mainly in Brazil's Pantanal, and also in Bolivia and Paraguay.
They prefer palm savannas and tropical forests where they can find their favorite food, the nuts of the acuri and bocaiuva palms.

Diet

Their diet mainly consists of palm nuts, seeds, fruits, and occasionally small plants.
They use their strong beaks to crack hard shells and access the nutritious contents inside.

Nesting

They nest in tree cavities or cliffs and are monogamous, often forming lifelong pair bonds.
Usually, a pair lays 1–2 eggs, and both parents care for the chicks until they fledge at around 3 months old.

Size & Lifespan

The largest flying parrot species, measuring up to 100 cm (3.3 ft) in length and weighing around 1.2 kg (2.6 lbs).
In the wild they can live 50 years or more and in captivity, they can live up to 60 years with proper care.
